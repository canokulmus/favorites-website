In Time

In Time is a 2011 American science fiction action film directed by Andrew Niccol and starring Justin Timberlake, Amanda Seyfried, Cillian Murphy, Olivia Wilde, Alex Pettyfer, and Vincent Kartheiser. The film was released on October 28, 2011, by 20th Century Fox. The film was a box office bomb, grossing $37 million worldwide against a $28 million budget.

In the near future, time has become the currency and the rich live forever while the poor, like Will Salas, have only a short time to live. Determined to earn more time, Will takes a job as a highly paid "timekeeper" for the rich. When he discovers that the system is corrupt, Will has to fight to change the system for all those who are being exploited.
