Escape From Alcatraz

Escape from Alcatraz is a 1979 American prison drama film directed by Don Siegel and starring Clint Eastwood, Patrick McGoohan, Roberts Blossom, Jack Thibeau, Fred Ward, and Larry Hankin. The screenplay by Richard Tuggle is based on the 1963 non-fiction book of the same name by J. Campbell Bruce and J. William Knaus. The film is about the 1962 escape from Alcatraz Federal Penitentiary by Frank Morris and brothers John and Clarence Anglin. The film was a box office success, grossing $44 million worldwide, and received positive reviews from critics.
