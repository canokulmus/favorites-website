Red Dead Redemption

Red Dead Redemption is a Western-themed action-adventure game developed by Rockstar San Diego and published by Rockstar Games. It is the third game in the Red Dead series, and was released for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 consoles in May 2010. An enhanced version for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One was released in October 2018, and a Microsoft Windows version was released in November 2019.
